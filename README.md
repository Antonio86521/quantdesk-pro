# 📊 QuantDesk Pro

**Professional Quantitative Finance Dashboard built in Python**

QuantDesk Pro is a full-stack financial analytics platform that allows users to analyze portfolios, price derivatives, explore volatility surfaces, run Monte Carlo simulations, and screen markets — all in one interactive dashboard.

## 🧠 Features

### 📊 Portfolio Analytics
- P&L tracking and performance metrics (Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar)
- Alpha/Beta vs benchmark
- Risk metrics (VaR, CVaR)
- Technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands)

### ⚠️ Risk & Attribution
- Rolling volatility, Sharpe, beta, correlation
- Historical & parametric VaR / CVaR
- Stress testing scenarios
- Correlation and covariance analysis

### 📐 Derivatives Pricing
- Black-Scholes model with full Greeks
- Binomial tree pricing (European & American)
- Monte Carlo option pricing
- Implied volatility solver
- Live options chain data

### 🌊 Volatility Surface
- Volatility smile by expiry
- ATM implied volatility term structure
- 2D heatmaps and 3D surfaces

### 🎲 Monte Carlo & Strategy Lab
- GBM price simulations
- Monte Carlo vs Black-Scholes convergence
- Option strategy payoff diagrams:
  - Straddle, Strangle, Spreads, Butterfly, Iron Condor
- Risk and breakeven analysis

### 🔬 Screener & Watchlist
- Multi-ticker market snapshot
- RSI and momentum signals
- 52-week high/low proximity
- Volume and volatility filters
